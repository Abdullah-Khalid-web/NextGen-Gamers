FAQ & Rules

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NextGen Gamers?

NextGen Gamers is a sub-brand of the BUITEMS Developers' Club dedicated to promoting online gaming and organizing competitive tournaments.

How can I register my team for a tournament?

To register your team, visit the registration page on our website and fill out the required details for each player or if you not find any registration page you can contact us dirEctly .

What games do you support for tournaments?

We currently support popular games like Valorant, PUBG, Dota 2, Taken and Call of Duty. Check our events page for a complete list.

Are there any registration fees?

Its Depend on the games, Some games are free and some has a minimal registration fee to participate in our tournaments. The fee will be mentioned on the event details page.

What are the rules for participating in tournaments?
  1. All players must adhere 10 mint before the tournament schedule strictly.
  2. No use of cheats, hacks, or unauthorized tools.
  3. Maintain respectful behavior towards all participants and organizers.
  4. Teams must have at least 5 players, with one designated leader.
  5. Follow the game-specific rules provided before the tournament.
