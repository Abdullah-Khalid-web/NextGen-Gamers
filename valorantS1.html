About Game

Online Valorant Tournament starts from 18th January, organized by BDC NextGen Gamers, where intense competitive matches await! Each match follows standard Valorant rules: a team must secure 13 wins or more, with a minimum 2-round difference to claim victory. Matches are scheduled twice daily, at 11:00 PM and 12:00 AM, so be sure to arrive 10 minutes early.
