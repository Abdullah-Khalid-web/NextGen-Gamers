About NextGen Gamers

A sub-brand of the BUITEMS Developers' Club, promoting online video games and tournaments.

Our Mission

NextGen Gamers is dedicated to fostering the gaming community by organizing exciting and competitive online gaming tournaments. We aim to provide a platform for gamers to showcase their skills, build friendships, and elevate eSports in the region.

Who We Are

As a sub-brand of the BUITEMS Developers' Club, we combine our technical expertise with a passion for gaming. Our team of developers and gamers work together to create seamless and fun tournament experiences.

What We Offer

  • Regular online gaming tournaments.
  • Community events and meetups.
  • Prizes and recognition for winners.
  • A platform to connect with other gaming enthusiasts.
